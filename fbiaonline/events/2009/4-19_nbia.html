The Florida Business Incubation Association (FBIA) is joining forces once again with the National Business Incubation Association (NBIA) to offer a conference scholarship to:

NBIA’s 23rd International Conference on Business Incubation “Building on Best Practices”
April 19-22, 2009
Kansas City, Missouri This scholarship is available to first-time NBIA conference attendees only and is limited to the cost of the early bird registration for the conference. Scholarship Eligibility Criteria: 1) Applicant must be a first-time NBIA conference attendee. 2) Applicant must be currently working in the Business Incubation or Economic Development field. 3) Applicant must be able to cover all travel costs associated with attending. 4) Applications must be submitted no later then 5:00 pm on March 11th, 2009.

***Only one scholarship will be awarded*** To apply for this scholarship opportunity, please complete the application form and send it to: mwasserm@mail.ucf.edu or fax it to 407-823-3299, Attn: Melissa Wasserman, by COB Mar 11th, 2009. Application Form (PDF)
